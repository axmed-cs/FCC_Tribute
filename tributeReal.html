Biography

  • Barack Obama served as the 44th President of the United states of America.
  • He was born in Honululu, Hawaii.
  • In the year of 1983 he graduated with a B.A. in political science from Columbia University.
  • 5 years later he enrolled in the Harvard Law school in 1988.
  • He served for two presidency terms starting from 2009 and ending in 2017.
  • Barack Obama was the first African American President in the history of the United States.
